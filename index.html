FALSE PIRATES

Established in 2009, False Pirates began as a digital sanctuary for a group of friends bound by shared experiences. Over the years, our Messenger threads have become a living archive of our lives—capturing every laugh, every challenge, and every milestone. We are a collective built on the strength of our past and the shared vision of our future.

What We Do

Fishing

Fishing isn't just a hobby for us; it’s where the False Pirates began. Whether we’re out on the water as anglers or gathering to share stories, these moments are the foundation of our crew. This group was born from a love for the sea and the patience it takes to land the perfect catch.

Drunken

A pirate's life isn't complete without a toast to the crew. For the False Pirates, being a 'drinker' is about the celebration of brotherhood. Since 2009, our best stories and deepest laughs have happened over a cold drink, proving that the strongest bonds are often forged during our simplest celebrations.

Gamer

Strategic thinkers and competitive players. From late-night Clash of Clans wars to conquering the latest online titles, gaming is the digital glue that keeps the False Pirates connected. We play hard, we win together, and we never back down from a challenge.
